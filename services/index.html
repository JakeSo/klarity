Why Klarity

Klarity Restaurant Advisors was founded on a simple premise: most restaurant problems are not mysteries — they have clear causes and proven solutions. What struggling and scaling restaurants alike lack is an experienced outside perspective to identify those causes and the accountability structure to act on them.

Our lead advisor brings over 37 years of hands-on experience operating, growing, and turning around restaurant concepts across multiple formats and markets. That background informs every engagement — from the questions we ask in the initial assessment to the recommendations we stand behind during implementation. Based in Charlotte, NC, we work with independent operators, regional groups, and multi-unit chains throughout the Southeast and nationally.
